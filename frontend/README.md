# Sprout — Project Documentation v2.0

> **Sprout is the first savings app where what you build outlives you. Your discipline becomes your child's inheritance — not just money, but a living record of every sacrifice you made to get there.**

---

## What Is Sprout?

Sprout turns savings into a living, growing digital tree. As you save, your tree evolves — from a tiny seed to a legendary ancient tree. But Sprout is more than a personal savings tool. It's a legacy platform.

Parents can build a **Family Tree** entirely for their child — depositing money, adding milestone memories, and watching a tree grow on their child's behalf. When the child comes of age, they receive everything: the money, the tree at its current level, inherited traits that tell their parent's story, and every memory ever added. Then the parent lets go. The gift is complete.

---

## Core Philosophy

- **Privacy first, always.** No public profiles. No financial surveillance. Every tree belongs exclusively to its owner.
- **Invisible technology.** No wallets, no seed phrases, no crypto jargon. Just email and a magic link.
- **Permanence matters.** Memories are stored on IPFS forever. The legacy outlasts any app.
- **The parent's role has a beautiful ending.** Inheritance is not loss — it's graduation.

---

## Privacy Model

| Tree Type              | Who Can See It                              |
| ---------------------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| Personal Savings Tree  | Only you. Always.                           |
| Parent's Family Tree   | Only the parent, while building it.         |
| Child's Inherited Tree | Only the child, after inheritance. Forever. |

There are no public profiles. No strangers can view your savings. No ongoing surveillance between parent and child after inheritance. The parent builds a legacy, then trusts the child with it completely.

### Why This Model

- If you don't want anyone knowing your finances, your personal tree is completely private.
- If you want to build something for your child, your family tree is private while you build it.
- If you don't want your parent watching you forever, the inherited tree becomes yours alone after inheritance.
- Your legacy lives on through visible traits — golden leaves, deep roots, full bloom — without revealing any financial figures.
- Inheritance is the beautiful finish line. The parent's role ends there, by design.

## NFT Evolution Levels

Your tree is a living digital heirloom that evolves as your savings grow. Each level change updates the tree's image immediately — stored permanently, forever.

| Level | Name           | Savings Required |
| ----- | -------------- | ---------------- |
| 1     | Seed           | $0 – $49         |
| 2     | Sprout         | $50 – $199       |
| 3     | Young Plant    | $200 – $499      |
| 4     | Flowering      | $500 – $999      |
| 5     | Small Tree     | $1,000 – $2,499  |
| 6     | Large Tree     | $2,500 – $4,999  |
| 7     | Golden Tree    | $5,000 – $9,999  |
| 8     | Legendary Tree | $10,000+         |

### The Dormancy Mechanic

If savings drop below a level due to a withdrawal, the tree enters a **dormant state** rather than resetting. It shows visible stress — browning leaves, drooping branches — while a ghost silhouette shows its recorded peak. _"This tree reached Golden. It's recovering."_ When savings grow back, the tree recovers fully. This keeps the metaphor honest while preserving progress.

## Core Features

### 1. Email Signup

Users enter their email and receive a magic link. Click it and they're in. Behind the scenes, Web3Auth creates an embedded wallet. The user never knows. No passwords. No seed phrases. No crypto knowledge required.

### 2. Personal Savings Tree _(Private — Only You)_

Your personal tree is completely separate from any family tree. It reflects your own savings journey and is visible to no one but you — not your parents, not your children.

**How it works:**

- Click "Add Money", enter an amount, confirm.
- Your first deposit grows your Seed tree.
- Every deposit checks if you've reached a new level.
- If a deposit triggers a level-up, a full-screen animation celebrates the moment.

**Withdrawal:** Withdraw anytime. Your money comes back to you. Your tree enters dormancy at its current level and recovers when you save again.

### 3. Parent's Family Tree _(Private — Only Parent)_

A separate tree that parents create entirely for their child. The money saved here is a gift — it is not the parent's to withdraw. It belongs to the child and will transfer at inheritance.

**How it works:**

- Parent creates a family tree, adds the child's name and an inheritance date.
- Parent saves money into this tree (completely separate from their personal tree).
- Tree grows through the same 8 levels.
- At each $500 milestone, parent can add a memory.
- The child never sees this tree until the inheritance event.

**Important:** At the moment of family tree creation, parents confirm: _"This money is for [child's name], not you."_ The savings are locked as a gift from the moment of deposit.

### 4. Memory System

Memories are the emotional core of the family tree. They transform savings into a scrapbook of love.

- At each $500 savings milestone, the parent can add a memory.
- Upload a photo (stored on IPFS — permanently, forever).
- Add a short note (150 characters).
- Each memory is timestamped with the savings milestone it was added at.
- Memories transfer to the child at inheritance and become part of their tree's story forever.

### 5. Trait Inheritance System

When the child receives their inherited tree, it carries **visible traits** that tell their parent's savings story. Traits are earned by _how_ the parent saved, not just _how much_.

| Parenting Behavior              | Trait on Child's Tree                               |
| ------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------- |
| Saved every week for 1+ year    | 🌿 **Deep Roots** — thick, visible root system      |
| Saved consistently for 5+ years | 🪵 **Ancient Bark** — weathered, wise-looking trunk |
| Reached Level 7 or higher       | ✨ **Golden Canopy** — gold-tipped leaves           |
| Added 10 or more memories       | 🌸 **Full Bloom** — flowers across the branches     |
| Never missed an auto-save       | 🌲 **Steady Growth** — perfectly symmetrical shape  |
| Built multiple children's trees | 🌱 **Grove Maker** — small saplings at the base     |

A child can look at their tree and _read_ their parent's story without ever seeing a dollar amount. That is legacy made visible.

### 6. Inheritance Event _(The Beautiful Moment)_

**When it happens:** The parent sets a specific inheritance date when creating the family tree (a birthday, a graduation — whatever feels right). The smart contract auto-triggers on that date. No parent action required on the day itself.

**30-day warning:** The parent receives a notification 30 days before the inheritance date — a final window to add one last memory.

**What transfers to the child:**

- All money saved in the family tree.
- The tree at its current level.
- All visible traits earned by the parent.
- Every memory ever added.

**What the parent receives:** A **Legacy Complete Certificate** — a beautifully designed keepsake commemorating their role. It includes their name, the child's name, years spent building, number of memories added, the highest level reached, and the date of inheritance. The parent can save it or share a public card that says: _"Today I passed a living tree to [Name]. A legacy 8 years in the making."_ No financial details are ever shared.

**After inheritance:** The parent loses access to the child's tree permanently. This is the point. The gift is unconditional.

### 7. Child's Inherited Tree _(Private — Only Child)_

The child's inherited tree is theirs and theirs alone from the moment of inheritance.

**What the child sees:**

- Their tree at the level it reached under their parent's care.
- Visible traits on the tree, each with a soft label explaining what it means: _"Deep Roots — your parent saved every week for 3 years."_
- A scrollable memory scrapbook — every photo and note their parent added, in chronological order.
- Their current savings amount (starting from what was transferred).
- The ability to keep growing the tree with their own savings through all 8 levels.

The parent cannot see any of this. Ever again. That is the gift's final form.

### 8. Auto-Save Rules

Set it and forget it. Rules like _"Save $10 every Friday"_ or _"Save $50 on the 1st"_ trigger automatic deposits using sponsored gas — the user pays nothing.

Rules are stored privately and are only visible to the tree owner. Auto-saves count toward the "Steady Growth" trait for family trees.

### 9. Savings Goals

- **Personal Goals:** Private to you. Track progress toward things that matter.
- **Family Goals:** Parents can set goals on a family tree (private to the parent while building).
- **Inherited Goals:** After inheritance, the child can set their own goals on their inherited tree.

### 10. Sponsored Gas

All transactions are gas-sponsored. Users pay $0 in blockchain fees. This is invisible to the user — they experience Sprout as a normal app, not a crypto product.

## The Inheritance Announcement _(Optional)_

After the inheritance event completes, the parent can optionally share a single public card. The card reveals nothing financial — no amounts, no tree details. Just:

_"Today I passed a living tree to [Name]. A legacy 8 years in the making."_

This is Sprout's viral loop. Every inheritance event becomes a potential new user. Opt-in only, and entirely non-financial.

## Language Guide

Sprout is not a crypto app. It's a savings and legacy app that happens to use blockchain infrastructure. The language should reflect that.

| Never Say                | Always Say                                      |
| ------------------------ | ----------------------------------------------- |
| NFT                      | Living tree / digital heirloom                  |

| Smart contract           | (never mention)                                 |
| IPFS                     | Stored permanently, forever                     |
